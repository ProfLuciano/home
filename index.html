Bio

I am an Associate Professor in the Computer Science Department at Universidade Federal de Pernambuco. In addition to my experience in academia, I also held different positions in industry research labs as: Visiting Scholar Researcher at Google AI (formerly Google Research); Research Scientist at IBM Research and AT&T Research Labs; and PhD summer intern at Yahoo! Research (Europe and USA). My research interests include web mining, text mining, natural language processing and data analytics.
